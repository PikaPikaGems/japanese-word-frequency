## Interpretation Notes

- **Advantage score**: difference in mean normalized rank between other sources and this group. Higher = this group ranks the word more frequently by a larger margin.
- **Slice of Life / Anime**: many top distinctive words are female character names (ユカリ, タマミ, カズミ) and school-life vocabulary (席替え = seat shuffle, 追試 = makeup exam).
- **Visual Novels**: top distinctive words skew toward adult content vocabulary — a known characteristic of the VN corpus.
- **YouTube**: top distinctive words are practical/DIY terms (画角 = camera angle/framing, コンセント = electrical outlet, 付箋 = sticky note, レジュメ = résumé/handout). YouTube tutorials and how-to content drive this vocabulary.
- **Dictionary / Balanced**: top distinctive words are classical and archaic terms (なむ, 拾遺, 若紫 from Tale of Genji, 雅楽 = court music). Dictionaries include headwords that almost never appear in modern media.
