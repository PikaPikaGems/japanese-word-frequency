# Task

## First Task

- get the top 25,000 words only
- put in a CSV file, 2 columns only: term, reading frequency

## Second Task

- get the top 25,000 words
- filter those words where the "kana frequency" is is smaller (higher frequency rank) than "reading frequency"
- put in CSV file 4 columns, term reading frequency kana_frequency

# Example Format (input)

```
term	reading	frequency	kana_frequency
の	の	1
は	は	2
に	に	3
を	を	4
と	と	5
も	も	6
が	が	7
で	で	8
から	から	9
だ	だ	10
する	する	11
```
