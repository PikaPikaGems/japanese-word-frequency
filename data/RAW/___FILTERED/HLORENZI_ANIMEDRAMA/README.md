# HLORENZI_ANIMEDRAMA — hlorenzi Jisho-open Anime/Drama Rankings

## Source

- **GitHub:** https://github.com/hlorenzi/jisho-open/blob/main/backend/src/data/word_rankings_animedrama.txt

## Description

Word frequency rankings derived from anime and drama subtitles, used by the jisho-open dictionary project.

**Author:** hlorenzi
**Data type:** subtitles (anime and drama)
**Coverage:** ~100,000 entries

## Format (plain text)

One word per line, ordered by frequency rank. Line 1 is the most frequent word (rank 1). No header row.

## DATA.csv

Top 25,000 words by frequency rank. Columns: `WORD`, `FREQUENCY_RANKING` (1 = most frequent).
